“the adventures began to-day,” so ran the words of unwisdom. “while i was clambering around on the mountain above the ocoee opening, _zip!_ came a bullet--yes, an indubitable leaden bullet fired from a gun--near enough to make me dodge. what will you think of me when i write it down in muddy black ink on white paper that i hid behind a tree! i did, you know, and immediately had plenty of reasons for being thankful that the tree was big enough to cover me, and thick enough through to stop a rifle-bullet.
