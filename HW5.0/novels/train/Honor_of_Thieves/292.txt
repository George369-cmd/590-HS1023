just as no man is ever successful in anything without due cause, so _per contra_ few sailormen are down on their luck except through some peculiar trait of incapacity. so that on your unpopular ship, be she tramp-steamer, or eke weeping wind-jammer, you do not get much pick of a crew. you have to put up with what other people have left, and it does not take you long to learn that your beauties have not been rejected for their excellences.
