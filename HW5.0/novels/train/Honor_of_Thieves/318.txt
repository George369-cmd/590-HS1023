but the fellow’s threats had not been without their result. every hand on the _port edes_’ deck had heard them distinctly, and disquiet arose under the belts of nine out of ten. the mates grew nervous and the men inattentive; and, from the bridge, captain kettle’s voice and whistle kept ringing out with biting clearness. as it was, only one man attempted to put the warning into practical effect. he was a miserable, half-clad wretch, a coal-trimmer by rating, already repentant of the spell of physical toil which he had signed on for.
