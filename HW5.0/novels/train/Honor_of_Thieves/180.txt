“ah,” said onslow, “so the new and valuable steamer, which, though over-insured, is likely to be reported lost, is evidently to have a consignment of specie on board. £500,000 i fancy you mentioned as the figure in the billiard-room this morning. well, if one is going in for robbery—or piracy, i suppose it would turn out to be in this instance—there’s nothing like a large _coup_. it’s your niggler who usually fails, and gets laid by the heels. drive on, and be a little more explicit.”
