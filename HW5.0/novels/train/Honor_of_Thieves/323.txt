“well, bless me!” retorted the donkeyman; “who’d miss you if you was killed—always supposing you weren’t wanted for our furnaces? here, get up, you half-baked scum of the workhouse, and tumble below. thank your stars the old man hasn’t seen you from the bridge. but don’t give me any more of your lip, or i’ll report you to him and the chief to boot. now, _mosey_.”
