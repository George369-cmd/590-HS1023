“_now!_” tregarvon commanded, setting the pace for the charge; but panic was before them. just as the man was withdrawing his arm a deep groan shuddered upon the stillness. with a cry that was like the snarl of a cornered animal, the man leaped up and flung out his arms as if to ward a blow. at that the huddled figure kneeling among the sunken graves groaned again, following the groan with a terrified, “_oh_, my lordy!” when he saw the man at the boiler head.
