“‘_port edes_, of london,’” hank read. “_port edes_? i seem to know that name.” he swung his long legs down over the cabin doorway, and sat staring at his companion with open-mouthed wonder. “hallo, nutt!” he said, “what’s wrong now! i haven’t seen you wear that kind o’ face before. you couldn’t look pleaseder if i’d said your rich uncle had gone dead. there’s no pards of ours aboard of her, is there?”
